## English

I am a Computer Science student and an independent Android developer. My mission is to build high-quality digital tools that empower the Odia-speaking community without financial barriers.

> **"Learning a language especially one's mother tongue should not depend on subscriptions or ads."**

- 🛠 **Technical Excellence:** I specialize in Android development using Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. **I actively leverage AI tools to optimize my code, improve UI/UX, and ensure my apps meet modern performance standards.**
- 🚀 **Featured Apps:** - **Odia Bhasa:** A comprehensive learning platform for Odia students.
  - **Plyxo Games:** A multi-game hub localized for a global audience.
  - **Atomic Guru & Ganita Bingya:** Educational apps focused on Science and Mathematics.
- 🎓 **Education:** Diploma in Computer Science (SCTE&VT, Odisha).
